Choose Your Monster

To use this calculator, you will need to download the Loot Logger plugin for RuneLite. This calculator will retrieve only the information for the specific monster and show you the drop rates for each item.
To do this:
1. Right-click the screenshot button (top right of RuneLite) and choose Open Screenshot Folder.
2. In the file explorer window, open the runelite folder, then npc.
3. Type in the name of the monster and open that folder.
The calculator will then automatically collect the drops and display them in a drop-rate table.
